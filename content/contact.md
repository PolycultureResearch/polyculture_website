## What to Expect

After you reach out, here's what happens:

1. **Initial conversation** — We'll schedule a 30-minute call to discuss your situation, challenges, and goals. No commitment, no sales pitch—just a conversation.
2. **Discovery** — If it seems like a good fit, I'll ask more detailed questions about your data, team, and objectives. This might involve looking at your current setup.
3. **Proposal** — Based on discovery, I'll put together a clear scope, timeline, and pricing for your project.
4. **Decision** — Take your time. Good partnerships are built on mutual fit, not pressure.

## Common Questions

**What size companies do you work with?**
Primarily companies between $5M-$30M in annual revenue—big enough to need sophisticated analytics, but not yet ready for a full data team.

**Do you work with early-stage startups?**
Sometimes, particularly if there's a strong sustainability focus or interesting data challenges. Happy to chat and see if it makes sense.

**What's your availability?**
I typically take on 2-3 projects at a time to ensure each client gets focused attention. Current availability varies—reach out and I'll let you know my timeline.

**Do you offer ongoing support?**
Yes. Many clients start with a project and then move to a retainer for ongoing support, ad-hoc analysis, and new initiatives.
