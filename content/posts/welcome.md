## What to expect

Here you'll find posts covering:

- **Practical analytics guidance** — How to build dashboards that actually get used, metrics that drive decisions, and data infrastructure that scales with your business
- **Sustainability measurement** — From carbon accounting to supply chain transparency, how to quantify environmental impact in meaningful ways
- **Lessons from the field** — Real experiences from building data functions at startups and conducting environmental research

## Why "Polyculture"?

In agriculture, polyculture means growing multiple crops together—creating resilient, productive systems that mimic natural ecosystems. It's the opposite of monoculture, where you optimize for a single output at the expense of everything else.

I think about analytics the same way. The best data practices aren't just about maximizing one metric. They're about building systems that help you understand your business holistically—the revenue *and* the retention, the growth *and* the sustainability.
