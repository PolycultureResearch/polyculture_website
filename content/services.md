## How We Work Together

### 1. Discovery

We start with a conversation to understand your business, your data challenges, and your goals. I'll ask questions about your current infrastructure, team capabilities, and what success looks like.

### 2. Build

Based on the discovery phase, I develop a scope and approach. Work happens in focused sprints with regular check-ins. You'll see progress weekly, not monthly.

### 3. Enable

The goal isn't to create dependency—it's to build capability. Every engagement includes documentation, training, and knowledge transfer so your team can maintain and extend the work.
